Product Price Checker

Display Customer Check Product Price By Barcode Only.

What is the Module Function?

The Product Price Checker module provides a seamless and efficient way for customers to check product prices simply by scanning a barcode. With this module, customers can instantly view the fixed product price along with any applicable discounts or promotions, ensuring complete transparency in pricing. This module is perfect for retail stores, supermarkets, and warehouses, helping customers check prices quickly and efficiently without needing assistance from store staff.

Key Features ?

  • Fixed Price & Discount Display – Shows the exact product price after applying discounts, offers, or loyalty rewards.
  • User-Friendly Interface – Simple and intuitive, ideal for self-service kiosks or in-store use.
  • Supports Multiple Barcode Formats – Works with EAN-13, UPC, and custom barcode formats in Odoo.
  • Real-Time Integration – Directly fetches price details from Odoo, ensuring accuracy.
